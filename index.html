Our Roots

A tree planted by a grandfather.
A promise kept by two friends.

In 1962, our founder's grandfather — Thatha — planted his first mango sapling on a quiet one-hectare plot. He didn't do it for profit. He believed that a tree, like a family, grows better when you give it patience, love, and the right soil.

Sixty years later, those same trees stand tall. The farm has never once used a synthetic pesticide. The soil remembers every season. And the mangoes — they still taste exactly the way Thatha intended.

Flying Mangoes is our way of honoring that legacy — the purest, most honest fruit from this family farm, directly to yours.
